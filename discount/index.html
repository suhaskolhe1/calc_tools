How to Calculate Discount?

To calculate the discount price, you first calculate the savings amount by multiplying the original price by the discount percentage. Then, subtract the savings from the original price.

Example Calculation

Original Price: $100, Discount: 20%

  • Savings: $100 * 0.20 = $20
  • Final Price: $100 - $20 = $80
