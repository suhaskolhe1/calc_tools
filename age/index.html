What is an Age Calculator?

An Age Calculator is a simple online tool that helps you determine your exact age based on your date of birth. While it's easy to know how many years old you are, calculating the exact number of months and days can be tricky due to varying month lengths and leap years. This tool does the math for you instantly.

How is Age Calculated?

Age is calculated by finding the difference between the current date and your birth date. The calculation takes into account:

  • The number of full years passed.
  • The remaining full months after the last birthday.
  • The remaining days in the current month.
  • Leap years (years with 366 days) are automatically adjusted for accuracy.

Example Calculation
